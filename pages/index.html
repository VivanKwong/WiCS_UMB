Mission Statement

The Women in Computer Science (WiCS) Club provides a place to celebrate and empower women and non-binary individuals in technology. This club is also open to all genders, and aims to foster community amongst those who come from historically marginalized backgrounds in tech. The goal is to hold talks, workshops, recruiting events, and partner with CS-related clubs at nearby universities for conferences and other events.
